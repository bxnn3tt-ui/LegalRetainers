## About LegalRetainers

LegalRetainers delivers pre qualified legal cases directly to plaintiff law firms. Unlike traditional lead generation that sends raw inquiries, LegalRetainers focuses on clients who have already:

1. **Signed representation agreements**: The client has completed a signed retainer
2. **Been screened for eligibility**: Basic case fit has already been reviewed
3. **Completed intake documentation**: Core intake details are included at delivery
4. **Given documented consent to contact**: The file includes consent for law firm outreach

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between a lead and a signed case?
A lead is an inquiry from someone who may need legal help. A signed case includes a client who has already completed a representation agreement and moved further through intake.

### What types of cases do you currently provide?
The live catalog currently includes motor vehicle accidents, SSDI and SSI disability claims, slip and fall cases, and dog bites and animal attacks.

### How quickly can I receive cases?
Most approved signed cases are delivered within 24 to 48 hours.

### Are the clients already signed?
Yes. Each delivered matter includes a signed representation agreement.

### What documentation is included with each case?
Each case includes the signed agreement, intake details, and supporting records available at delivery.

### Do you serve firms nationwide?
Yes. LegalRetainers works with plaintiff law firms across the United States.

### What makes LegalRetainers different from lead generation services?
LegalRetainers is built around signed case delivery, not raw lead volume. The goal is to reduce the gap between inquiry and retained client.
